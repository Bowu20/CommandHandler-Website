What is CommandHandler?

Well I'm (@Bowu20) so glad you asked! I developed CommandHandler originally to just handle commands, aliases, multi-prefixes. But it's become so much more. It's not an all in one toolkit if you will! Having a multitude of future and current modules and features! I'm taking suggestions when and where they're given! In fact, you can go right now and submit a suggestion! /Bowu20/CommandHandler-Public

I want to essentially turn what was to help me make small simple bots for clients and myself, into a bot that I release to the public for sale! My goal is to have every command imaginable at an affordable price! The first version of the bot will NOT likely be available for the clients to self host. I first want to find or create a "perfect" protection with authentication. Don't fret about the authentication. If the authentication servers ever were decommissioned, I would release a version without the authentication built in.

Developed by @Bowu20 to handle DiscordGo "easier".
